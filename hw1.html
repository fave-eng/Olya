Regular verbs: добавляем -ed → arrive → arrived, book → booked
Irregular verbs: особая форма V2 → take → took, go → went, buy → bought
Отрицание (−): didn't + V1 → didn't buy (не didn't bought!)
Вопрос (?): Did + subject + V1? → Did they arrive?
